About Me

I'm Ronaldo Footballer

I'M Cristiano Ronaldo, A football legend, I managed to conquer the world of soccer and fashion as well. I was Born In portugal, I Grew up in a Hometown Called Madeira.
When I was Twelve I moved to Lisbon, I always had a passion over football since I was Childhood.

I started Football Career as a youth player for Andorinha, I played for two years there, Later I went to C.D. Nacional. In (1997) I moved to Sporting CP Lisbon football Club.
Later in 2003 I signed for United. I improved a lot in there. I helped the club to win a lot of trophies which made the European and worlds best player in 2008.
I became the most expensive footballer when I made a transfer to Real Madrid in 2009. Until today I still plays for Read Madrid. I won The world best player awards thrice in My career so far. "Ronaldo: "I want to be one of the best ever"".

Football, also called association football or soccer, is a game involving two teams of 11 players who try to maneuver the ball into the other team's goal without using their hands or arms.
